Recognizing the Symptoms

Hyperhidrosis primarily affects specific areas of the body, known as focal hyperhidrosis. Common regions include:

  • Hands: Palmar hyperhidrosis leading to clammy hands.
  • Feet: Plantar hyperhidrosis causing slippery feet.
  • Underarms: Axillary hyperhidrosis resulting in visible sweat stains.
  • Face: Excessive facial sweating, often accompanied by blushing.

Emotional and Social Impact

People with hyperhidrosis may experience anxiety, embarrassment, and avoidance of social situations due to their condition.
