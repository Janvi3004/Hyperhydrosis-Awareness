Medical Interventions

There are several treatments available depending on the severity of hyperhidrosis:

  1. Topical Antiperspirants: Apply directly to affected areas to reduce sweating.
  2. Medications: Oral drugs to inhibit sweat gland activity.
  3. Botox Injections: Temporarily block sweat glands for up to 6 months.
  4. Surgery: Severe cases may require sympathectomy or gland removal.

Alternative Approaches

Natural remedies and lifestyle changes can complement medical treatments:

  • Herbal teas like sage tea.
  • Mindfulness and stress management practices.
  • Avoiding spicy foods and caffeine.
